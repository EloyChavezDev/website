##### Introduction
Microsoft recently announced the general availability of a new feature in Microsoft Teams : skin tone settings for emojis and reactions. This update allows users to personalize their digital interactions by choosing a skin tone that best represents them, helping to create a more inclusive and authentic experience.

---

##### Key features
- Emoji and reaction customization : Users can now choose a skin tone for their emojis and reactions to use everywhere in Microsoft Teams, including in meetings, chats, and channels. This customization helps better represent their physical appearance and express themselves more authentically in virtual interactions.

- Skin tone settings : To change skin tone settings, go to *Settings* > *Appearance and accessibility*, and select the desired option under *Skin tone*. This feature is available on Windows, Mac, web, Android, and iOS.

- Direct customization : Users can also set their skin tone directly in the emoji and reaction menus. To do this, simply hover over a message in a chat or select the *Emoji, GIFs & Stickers* button in the message box, then click on the *Customize* button next to the search box, choose the desired skin tone, and save.

- Single use : It is possible to send an emoji or reaction with a different skin tone without changing the default setting by right-clicking on an emoji or reaction on Windows, Mac, and Web, or by long-pressing on an emoji or reaction on Android and iOS.

##### User benefits
- Authentic representation : By allowing users to choose a skin tone for their emojis and reactions, Microsoft Teams helps enable more authentic and personalized representation in digital interactions.
- Inclusive experience : This feature fosters a more inclusive and engaging experience, where every user can feel represented and valued.
- Ease of use : Skin tone settings are consistent across devices, ensuring a seamless experience for users no matter what device they are using.
