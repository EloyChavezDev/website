##### Introduction
Since the arrival of Teams Direct Routing telephony, TLS certificates used by Microsoft servers are linked to the following root certification authority :
- Common Certificate Authority Name: Baltimore CyberTrust Root
- Thumbprint (SHA1): d4de20d05e66fc53fe1a50882c78db2852cae474

This certificate expires on May 12, 2025 and it is important that its replacement (*DigiCert Global Root G2*) is installed on your SBC Audiocodes in parallel.
- Common name of the certificate authority: DigiCert Global Root G2
- Thumbprint (SHA1): df3c24f9bfd666761b268073fe06d1cc8d4f82a4

![image](/images/blog/sbc/sbc_import_cert_000.png)

---

##### Prerequisites
**<u>Administrator role</u>**
- An administrator account to access the Audiocodes SBC.

**<u>Have the certificate</u>**
- The DigiCert Root G2 certificate is downloadable via https://cacerts.digicert.com/DigiCertGlobalRootG2.crt.

**<u>Others</u>**
- Schedule the installation during off-hours

---

##### Step 1 : Log in to the SBC

Connect to the SBC by opening your web browser to https://YOUR_IP_ADDRESS.

![image](/images/blog/sbc/sbc_audiocodes_import_cert_000.png)

---

##### Step 2 : Import the certificate <br/><br/>
<Notice type="info">The import does not require a restart but I recommend doing it during closed hours just in case!</Notice>

Click on *Setup*, *IP Network* then *Security* and *TLS Contexts*. Select the container into which to import the certificate and click on *Trusted Root Certificates*.

![image](/images/blog/sbc/sbc_audiocodes_import_cert_001.png)

Click *Import* and browse Windows File Explorer.

![image](/images/blog/sbc/sbc_audiocodes_import_cert_002.png)

Once the certificate is selected, it will be imported. 

![image](/images/blog/sbc/sbc_audiocodes_import_cert_003.png)

Validate that the serial number is *<u>033AF1E6A711A9A0BB2864B11D09FAE5</u>*.

![image](/images/blog/sbc/sbc_audiocodes_import_cert_004.png)

---

##### Conclusion
You now know how to import a certificate into an Audiocodes SBC. Note that the process would be the same with any other intermediate certificate.
